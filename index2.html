Amie Arias, the original owner of Vegan Van is who started it all.
Through Amie, our lives were changed and by 2013, the demand for vegan food was in high.
We then stepped in and joined Amie on the truck, learning to laugh a lot and our love of making vegan food.
When Amie left, we decided to take over. Through this, our passion of cooking began and never stopped.
We are Colorado natives who have been vegan for over 5 years, with our involvement in the community since 2012,
where we started cooking a community dinner called Chomp.



Just because ownership of Vegan Van has changed, doesn’t mean that the values haven’t.
